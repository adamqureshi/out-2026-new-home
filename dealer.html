Dealer FAQ

Questions dealers ask before they connect a feed

Simple answers on onboarding, feeds, inventory exposure, and the GPT rollout.

How do dealers get started?

Email us your dealership name, location, website, feed provider, and current Tesla inventory count. We’ll review the feed and coordinate onboarding.

What happens after we send the feed?

We ingest the feed into Only Used Tesla, display eligible inventory on the site, and market those listings to Tesla-focused shoppers.

Do we need to upload cars manually?

Usually no. The goal is to work from your existing feed so your inventory can stay updated more efficiently.

What dealers are the best fit?

Dealers with Tesla inventory are the strongest fit. If you also carry EV inventory, ask us what can be supported.

Are you really building a GPT experience?

Yes. Only Used Tesla is building AI-powered discovery tools, including GPT-based experiences, so shoppers can search dealer inventory through conversational interfaces as those tools roll out.

What does Only Used Tesla do beyond listing cars?

We help with marketplace exposure, Tesla-specific presentation, search visibility, paid promotion, and inventory marketing designed around Tesla shoppers.
