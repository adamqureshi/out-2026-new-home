Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Only Used Tesla collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you visit the Site, submit a form, contact us, or interact with our listings and services.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to onlyusedtesla.com and related pages, forms, landing pages, content, and services operated by Only Used Tesla (“Only Used Tesla,” “we,” “us,” or “our”).

2. Information we collect

We may collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • name, email address, phone number, and other contact details;
  • vehicle details, listing content, dealership information, pricing, photos, and message content;
  • information submitted through forms, dealer inquiries, cash-offer requests, or support requests; and
  • any other information you choose to send to us.

We may also collect certain information automatically, such as:

  • IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and general location derived from IP;
  • pages viewed, clicks, referring URLs, time on page, and other interaction data; and
  • cookie, analytics, and similar technology data used to understand and improve performance.

3. How we use information

We may use information we collect to:

  • operate, maintain, and improve the Site and our services;
  • respond to inquiries, support requests, dealer applications, and lead submissions;
  • publish or manage listings and related marketing content;
  • communicate with you about services, updates, campaigns, or opportunities relevant to your inquiry;
  • monitor usage, prevent abuse, detect fraud, and protect the Site; and
  • comply with legal obligations and enforce our policies.

4. How we may share information

We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • with service providers that help us host, analyze, market, secure, or operate the Site;
  • with relevant sellers, dealers, partners, or service providers when needed to respond to your inquiry or requested service;
  • when required by law, subpoena, court order, or to protect rights, safety, and property; and
  • in connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, sale of assets, or similar business transaction.

We do not currently sell personal information for money. We may, however, use service providers and advertising or analytics tools that process data on our behalf in connection with operating and improving the Site.

5. Cookies and analytics

We may use cookies, pixels, local storage, and similar technologies to remember preferences, understand user behavior, measure campaign performance, and improve the Site. You can manage many of these controls through your browser settings.

6. Data retention

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including to operate the Site, respond to inquiries, maintain records, resolve disputes, and satisfy legal obligations.

7. Your choices and rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to request access, correction, deletion, or limitation of certain personal information.

  • You may opt out of marketing emails by using the unsubscribe link or contacting us.
  • You may request updates or deletion of information you submitted by emailing us.
  • You may manage cookie preferences through your browser or device settings.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures intended to protect information under our control. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Children’s privacy

The Site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and delete it if appropriate.

10. Third-party links

The Site may link to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of those third parties, and your use of them is subject to their own policies.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time by posting a revised version on the Site. The “Effective” date above reflects the current version of this policy.

12. Contact us

Questions about this Privacy Policy or requests relating to your information may be sent to hello@onlyusedtesla.com.
