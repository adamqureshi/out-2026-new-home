Common questions.

Straight answers about pricing, ad length, cash offers, concierge help, reviews, Facebook group posting, and how Only Used Tesla works.

Why do you charge $27?

We charge $27 because we do not sell your data to make money from you. We are a small business and we charge a fair price. That is it.

How long does my ad last?

Your $27 ad lasts 30 days.

Can I get a cash offer for my Tesla?

Yes. We have a dealer network that can make you a cash offer or try to beat one.

Best move: get a cash offer from Carvana or CarMax first, then send it to us so a dealer in our network can try to beat it.
