What is OUT‑MATCH?

Three ways to use it

OUT‑MATCH is a Tesla-only concierge layer for both sides of the deal. Use it for a full buyer search, a second-look review on a live listing, or seller-side help when you want more structure than a raw self-serve listing.

I need help finding the right Tesla

Tell us the trim, budget, location, timeline, and must-haves. Your OUT Agent helps search, shortlist, and compare real options.

I already found a Tesla

Send the listing. OUT‑MATCH can pressure-test the price, review the deal structure, and flag questions worth asking before you sign.

I need help selling my Tesla

Get help with pricing, listing positioning, cash-offer comparison, buyer screening, video guidance, and the closing process without doing every step alone.

How the work gets done

AI-assisted, human-led

AI speeds up research

Use AI to summarize listings, organize buyer or seller answers, compare comps, and speed up Tesla-specific research.

Humans make the judgment calls

An OUT Agent decides what matters, what to ask next, how to position the car, and where someone should push or walk away.

Owners still control the media and final yes/no

The owner still approves the asking price, records the car, chooses the buyer, and decides whether to accept an offer.

Clear guardrails

OUT‑MATCH supports decision-making and process, but it is not legal, tax, lending, insurance, or state-licensed dealer/broker advice. Customers still make the final call.

Quick FAQ

Good questions for the beta

Is this one form for buyers, sellers, and future OUT Agents?

Yes. The intake uses logic. Choose buyer, seller, buy + sell, or future OUT Agent and the form only shows the questions that matter for that track.

Is OUT‑MATCH only for buyers?

No. Buyers can use OUT‑MATCH for search or second-look help, sellers can hire an OUT Agent for pricing, positioning, screening, and closing support, and future experts can apply from the same page.

Will OUT‑MATCH run ads for my Tesla?

Seller marketing help can be part of the scope, including where to post, how to frame the car, and whether paid promotion makes sense. If paid media is used, ad spend should be scoped separately.

Do you shoot the photos or video?

The owner should capture the car. OUT‑MATCH helps with the shot list, the talking points, and how to use the media better across the listing and outreach.

Can you help me compare a cash offer vs. trying to sell privately?

Yes. That is one of the clearest sell-side use cases: compare the quick-exit option with the upside and workload of a private sale.

Do you handle the payment or title transfer for me?

We can help you think through a cleaner process and better questions, but you still choose the payment method, title path, and any third-party services needed to close safely.
