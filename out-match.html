What is OUT‑MATCH?

Two ways to use it

OUT‑MATCH is a Tesla-only buyer support service. You can use it as a full concierge search, or as a second-look service when you already found a Tesla and want a sharper opinion before you buy.

I need help finding the right Tesla

Tell us the trim, budget, location, timeline, and must-haves. Your OUT Agent helps search, shortlist, and compare real options.

I already found a Tesla

Send the listing. OUT‑MATCH can pressure-test the price, review the deal structure, and flag questions worth asking before you sign.

How it works

Simple, Tesla-focused, and human-led

The beta is meant to feel lightweight for the buyer while still doing the real work behind the scenes.

Step 1

Start with a fit call

We define whether you need a full search, a second look, or help with one specific live deal.

Step 2

Your OUT Agent does the work

We research listings, compare prices, review options and warranty context, and call the seller or dealer if needed.

Step 3

You decide with more confidence

We help you negotiate, review paperwork for common issues, and move toward pickup or delivery with fewer surprises.

What's included

What a buyer gets for the beta fee

Every engagement is different, but the core service is built around the jobs buyers actually need help with.

Targeted Tesla search

Search support based on trim, battery, budget, region, accident history, mileage, and feature priorities.

Second-look review

A smarter pass on a Tesla you already found so you can spot weak pricing or missing details before you move.

Seller or dealer outreach

Help contacting the seller to clarify condition, extras, charging setup, paperwork, timing, and flexibility on price.

Negotiation support

Guidance on a realistic offer, talking points, and next-best options so you do not negotiate from a weak position.

Use AI to summarize listings, compare deal structure, surface red flags, and speed up Tesla-specific research.

Paperwork guidance

Common-sense review of purchase documents, fees, and deal terms to help you ask better questions before signing.

Excited to help more people get into Teslas

How the work gets done

AI-assisted, human-led

AI speeds up research

Use AI to summarize listings, organize seller answers, and compare deal terms faster than manual note-taking alone.

Humans make the judgment calls

An OUT Agent decides what matters, what to ask next, and where the buyer should push or walk away.

Scope can vary by deal

Some engagements are short second-look reviews. Others may involve sourcing, calls, negotiation, and closing support.

Clear guardrails

OUT‑MATCH supports decision-making, but it is not legal, tax, lending, or insurance advice. The buyer still makes the final call.

Quick FAQ

Good questions for the beta

How long does an engagement last?

There is not one fixed length. Some buyers only need a second-look review. Others need sourcing, outreach, negotiation, and support through signing.

Can OUT‑MATCH work with dealer or private-seller listings?

Yes. The service is meant to help on both sides, as long as the seller is responsive and the buyer wants support.

Do I have to use OUT‑MATCH before I find a car?

No. You can come in early for a full search or late for a second opinion on a Tesla you already found.
