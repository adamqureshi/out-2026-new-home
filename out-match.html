Quick FAQ

Simple answers

What is OUT‑MATCH Concierge?

It is a Tesla seller concierge service. I review your car, your VIN, the market, cash offers, and your options so you can make a cleaner decision.

How much does it cost?

$499 paid upfront before I start any work.

How long does it last?

A typical engagement lasts 10 days. That is usually enough to figure out the right direction. If more time is needed, renewal can be discussed after the first 10 days.

Do you buy the car from me?

No. I help you review your options. If a dealer cash offer makes sense, I can bring you that option. If advertising makes more sense, I can help you think through that path too.

Can you help me buy a Tesla too?

Sometimes, yes. Put that in your message or email me directly and I will tell you if it is a fit.
