What CarConnect AI does

Not another marketplace. A direct inventory connection layer for AI shopping.

Dealers already pay for listings. CarConnect AI is the dealer-facing layer for making inventory easier for AI shopping experiences to read, present, and route back to the store.

Connect the feed

Pull inventory, pricing, photos, and VDP links from your DMS or feed provider into one structured format.

Normalize the data

Clean up vehicle details, dealer contact points, and store metadata so AI tools can read the inventory clearly.

Launch the channel

Publish an AI-ready shopping layer that helps shoppers discover vehicles and land on your site, forms, and phone numbers.

Keep the store relationship

Use AI visibility to send qualified shoppers back to the dealership instead of handing the whole journey to a third-party listing site.

How onboarding works

From feed access to live beta in a few clear steps.

Simple enough to test quickly, structured enough to sell as a real dealer product.

Step 1

Kickoff and rooftop review

Review rooftops, goals, store sites, and which provider controls the inventory feed.

Step 2

Feed access and mapping

Pull vehicle data, pricing, photos, and URLs into one normalized format AI tools can use.

Step 3

QA and store setup

Verify VINs, pricing, VDP links, dealer contact points, and store metadata before launch.

Step 4

Launch and measure

Go live, route shoppers back to the store, and use the beta to learn what dealers will actually pay for.

Quick FAQ

Good questions for the beta

Is this a marketplace listing product?

No. CarConnect AI is positioned as a direct inventory connection layer for AI-assisted shopping, not another generic marketplace listing package.

Can I use this alongside the dealer page on Only Used Tesla?

Yes. This repo version is built so you can market CarConnect AI as a separate product while still keeping the Only Used Tesla dealer sign-up flow live.

What should I send to start?

Your dealer name, rooftop count, location, website, feed provider, and launch timeline are enough to kick off the conversation.
